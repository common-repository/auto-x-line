This plugin will publish posts automatically to your LINE Official Channel.

== Description ==
This plugin will publish posts automatically to your LINE official Channel. It also has basic features such as the log (latest 100), Post delay (by Cron), changing the post timing (Post immediately, post delay, not post).

* Connect your LINE Channel
* Logs of Post Sent Or Not
* Japanese and English Language Support
